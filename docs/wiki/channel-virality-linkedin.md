## Algorithm reality (2026)

- **Dwell time is the #1 hidden metric.** How long someone stares at your post before scrolling away matters more than likes.
- **Comments carry ~50× the algorithmic weight of likes.** Back-and-forth threaded discussions get 5.2× amplification vs a single top-level comment.
- **Comments from relevant industry experts are weighted 5–7× more than random-connection comments.** Therefore: engage with peers' posts *before* publishing your own — drives reciprocal weighted comments.
- **External links in the body kill reach by ~60%.** Always put the link in the first comment, never in the body.
- **Text-only posts get ~30% more reach than single-image posts** (image tax is real in 2026). But **document / PDF carousels = 6.60% engagement rate — the highest format on the platform.**
- **LinkedIn Brazil has 83.2M users** — LATAM is the #2 market on the platform.
- **Platform-wide engagement rate average: 3.85%** (up 44% YoY). your target: beat 3.85%.

## Hook structure

The first 3 lines before "...see more" do 100% of the work. If they fail, the post fails.

Winners in 2026 format:
1. **Personal narrative + unexpected outcome** — "I [did something]. [Result]. Here's what I learned." Gives the reader a reason to keep scrolling.
2. **News event + second-order implication** — "[Company] just [did X]. Everyone is focused on Y. The real story is Z."
3. **Specific stat + controversial reframe** — "R$ 1,5 bi em sports tech no Brasil em 2025. 92% foi recapitalização, não growth. O que isso realmente significa."

Avoid: generic questions, platitudes about AI, "thought leadership" phrases ("Here's why it matters...").

## Video spec

- **Duration**: 30–90 seconds sweet spot (60s is the median that performs)
- **Aspect**: 16:9 feed or 4:5 mobile-first (4:5 recommended in 2026 — higher hold rate)
- **Captions**: burned in (85% watch muted)
- **Production**: **selfie-style from phone works as well as produced content.** 2026 LinkedIn study: "Video with a real person on camera cuts through skepticism in a way text can't."
- **Hook**: must land in first 3 seconds
- **Completion rate target**: 60%+ — algo boosts videos that hit this

## Language strategy

- **Brazil-specific stories → PT-BR primary**, 1-line EN summary at the end for global followers
- **Global / product / AI-agent stories → EN primary**
- **Never duplicate: do not post the same content in both languages in separate posts.** Hurts both posts' reach.

Bilingual single-post format (works in 2026): 3 paragraphs PT-BR followed by "**In English:**" divider and 3 paragraphs EN. Keeps dwell time high (readers scroll through both).

## Posting cadence

- **2 posts/day maximum.** 3–4/day (current FALCAO rate) may actually be cannibalizing dwell time. **Quality over frequency.**
- Optimal time slots (Warsaw/CEST where you lives):
  - 10:00 Warsaw = 08:00 UTC = 05:00 BRT (early for BR, peak for EU)
  - 14:00 Warsaw = 12:00 UTC = 09:00 BRT = 08:00 ET (best overlap)
- Post → respond to every comment in the first 60 minutes. Golden hour. If no replies in 60min, the algo marks it dead and stops serving.

## Viral mechanic unique to LinkedIn

**The pre-publication engagement ritual.** 30 minutes BEFORE publishing, leave thoughtful comments on 3–5 peers' recent posts (industry heavyweights, engineers/investors you respects). This:
1. Warms the algo — your profile is "active" so your post gets initial distribution
2. Earns reciprocal comments from those peers (weighted 5–7× in algo)
3. Pulls their audiences into your notifications

Cost: 10 minutes. ROI: consistently 2–3× post reach vs cold publish.

## Anti-pattern checklist

- "Food for thought" / "My 2 cents"
- Any banned word list (see X playbook)
- External link in body (kills reach −60%)
- Asking for likes or shares explicitly
- Posting on Saturday morning Brazil time (dead zone for BR executive audience)

## Metrics to track

- **Engagement rate** per post (reactions + comments + reposts / impressions)
- **Comment-to-like ratio** (target ≥0.15; 1 comment per 7 likes = high-quality engagement)
- **Expert-comment count** (comments from followers you follows back)
- **Dwell signal proxy**: view-to-reaction ratio (higher = dwell time lower)

Floor: 2.0% engagement rate. Viral: ≥6.0%. Platform avg: 3.85%.
