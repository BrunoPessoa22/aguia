## Algorithm reality (Mosseri Apr 2026)

Three priority signals (official):
1. **Watch time** (Reels) / **dwell time** (Carousels)
2. **Likes per reach** — normalized, not raw likes
3. **Sends per reach** — the #1 signal, DM forwards are gold

Secondary: saves per reach, comments per reach.

**Mosseri's 2026 thesis: "authenticity is becoming infinitely reproducible."** The polished feed is dead. The bar shifts from *"can you create?"* to *"can you make something that only you could create?"* This has direct implications for visual style — see "Anti-AI-generic" section.

## Reels vs Carousels in 2026

| Metric | Reels | Carousels |
|---|---|---|
| Avg engagement rate | ~6% | ~10% |
| New follower reach | **2–3× Reels > Carousels** | lower |
| Existing audience engagement | lower | **Carousels > Reels** |
| Watch/dwell signal | watch time | swipe depth |

**Strategy:** Reels for discovery / new-follower growth. Carousels for existing-audience engagement and lead-magnet CTAs (newsletter, product).

## Reel spec (new priority for FALCAO)

- **Duration: 7–15 seconds** is the viral sweet spot (not 30s, not 60s — short wins in 2026)
- **Aspect**: 9:16 (1080×1920) always
- **Hook**: must land in first **1.5 seconds** (before caption overlay fully renders)
- **3-second hold rate**: target ≥60%. Reels with >60% hold rate at 3s get 5–10× total reach vs <40%.
- **Captions**: burned in, mobile-safe bottom center, high contrast
- **Trending audio**: use only if it genuinely fits the content. Trending audio boosts *discovery* but does NOT improve retention. Forced trend = algo penalty.
- **Cover**: first frame IS the cover by default; optimize it for the grid preview.

Narrative arc for 3-shot viral Reels:
- Shot 1 (0–2s): setup — the premise / stat / provocation
- Shot 2 (2–6s): tension — the obstacle or contradiction
- Shot 3 (6–15s): payoff — the resolution / call to action

## Carousel spec (upgraded)

- **Slides: 6–13** (was: 5). 6+ outperforms 5 in 2026.
- **Dimensions**: 1080×1350 (4:5 portrait — best engagement in feed) or 1080×1080 (square).
- **Two visual styles, rotate 50/50** (see `falcao/assets/design-system.md`):
  - **Typographic** (brandsdecoded-inspired): black bg + orange accent + condensed heavy type + numbered circles. For data-heavy / list / punchline content. **This is the anti-AI-generic style Mosseri is rewarding.**
  - **Cinematic**: full-bleed image + 35/65 text split. For narrative / emotional / story-driven.

Structure:
- Slide 1: hook (one line, type-dominant)
- Slides 2–N-1: content beats
- Last slide: CTA — newsletter link `intersecao.brunopessoa.com`, "link na bio", or question to invite DMs

## Anti-AI-generic visual rules

**Mosseri is down-ranking the "AI-generic" look** — cinematic 8K, hyperreal, polished-to-death. +30% search volume for hand-drawn, visible grain, wobbly lines, imperfection-as-deliberate.

Forbidden prompt phrases for Imagen 4:
- "cinematic 8K"
- "ultra realistic" / "hyperrealistic"
- "ultra detailed"
- "photo realistic"
- "professional studio lighting"

Preferred framing:
- Documentary / photojournalism
- 35mm film grain, Tri-X, Portra
- Editorial composition — rule of thirds, off-center, negative space
- Specific detail over generic — not "a stadium" but "empty tunnel before kickoff, one security guard, mid-shot"
- Medium contrast, not high-contrast glossy

## Viral mechanics unique to IG in 2026

1. **Broadcast Channels** — accounts >5K followers get explicit algo boost for having an active Broadcast Channel. X.XK qualifies. Open one titled **"Sports × AI × Brazil"**. Push 1 insight/day. Subscribers see it push-notified. Zero effort vs reach gain.
2. **Threads cross-post** — Meta prompts cross-posting at every upload. Zero cost; Threads discovery feeds back to IG follower growth. you has ZERO presence on Threads currently.
3. **Send-to-friend signal** — optimize for DM-worthiness. Posts that make someone think "I have to send this to X" rank highest. Data contrasts, surprising stats, named-personality-quotes work best.
4. **Story → Feed funnel** — polls/question boxes in Stories warm the audience before a big feed post. 20-30 min later, drop the Reel/Carousel on the same topic. Engagement compounds.
