# CB-Sales — Insight Patterns to capture

Call wiki-remember.sh when you observe any of these:

1. **Conversion pattern (DM → meeting → close — steps that consistently worked)**
   Title: `CB-Sales conversion — [segment] [step]`
   Content: customer segment, specific DM opening that got reply, meeting structure that moved to close, time-to-close, deal size. Extract the replicable template.

2. **Rejection pattern (3+ similar companies/segments rejecting with same reason)**
   Title: `CB-Sales rejection cluster — [segment or size]`
   Content: segment, n outreach, common rejection framing, whether it's pricing / timing / authority / fit, recommendation to deprioritize or adjust pitch.

3. **LinkedIn automation limit hit (cooldown, search cap, connect cap)**
   Title: `LinkedIn limit — [specific cap] [symptom]`
   Content: action (search/connect/DM/view), cap value, symptom (captcha/restriction/silent fail), workaround, recovery time.

4. **Budget discovery (decision-maker disclosing training/L&D budget)**
   Title: `Comp learning budget — [company size] [range]`
   Content: company size band (<100 / 100-500 / 500-2k / >2k), approximate budget range disclosed, budget-holder title, typical procurement cycle.

5. **Champion pattern (who internally advocates for CB)**
   Title: `CB champion profile — [role or dept]`
   Content: role/department, what triggered their advocacy (demo / free tier / Bruno's content), which objections they internally handled.

DO NOT write for every outreach sent or routine no-reply.
